Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper until well mixed. Optional: add 1 tbsp of water or milk.
  2. Heat the pan: Use medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs. Tilt to spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Add fillings when the edges are set but center is slightly runny.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette in half and slide onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, season if needed.
